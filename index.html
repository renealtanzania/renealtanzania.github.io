Message from Country Director

DN

Welcome to Reneal Tanzania. Our mission is to provide quality educational resources to communities across Tanzania, ensuring that learning continues even in areas with limited internet connectivity.

Through our offline educational platforms and computer labs, we're bridging the digital divide and empowering the next generation of learners. Since 2012, we have installed over 104 computer labs in 11 regions across Tanzania.

Together, we can continue to expand access to education and create opportunities for thousands of students. Thank you for your support.

David Nyangaka

Country Director, Reneal Tanzania
