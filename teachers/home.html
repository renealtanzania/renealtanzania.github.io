Teacher's Account

Super Users will provide other teachers on how to create their own lesson plan. Each teacher will have their own working area Account as long as they signed in to their Google Docs Account.

Way of Use (Skim of Work & Lesson Plan)

  • Google Docs Account Creation. The app will use Google Docs to help you manage your activities.
  • Manual file creation. The app will accept the file you create manually to help you manage your activities.
