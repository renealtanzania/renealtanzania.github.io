Training Overview

Our training programs are designed to equip teachers with the skills and confidence to use technology effectively in their classrooms, maximizing the impact of our computer lab installations.

Hands-On Learning

Practical, interactive training sessions that allow teachers to practice with the actual systems they'll be using in their schools.

Peer Collaboration

Training sessions foster collaboration among teachers, creating a supportive network for ongoing learning and resource sharing.

Ongoing Support

Continuous technical support and follow-up training ensure teachers feel confident and supported long after initial training.

Training Schedule

Trainings are typically conducted when new computer labs are installed or upon request from existing partner schools.

Phase 1: Lab Installation

During computer lab setup, we conduct initial training for super users and key staff members to ensure they can manage the system from day one.

Week 1-2 after installation

Phase 2: Teacher Orientation

Following super user training, we conduct comprehensive orientation sessions for all teaching staff to introduce them to the available resources.

Week 3-4 after installation

Phase 3: Ongoing Support

We provide continuous support through regular site visits, remote assistance, and refresher training sessions as needed throughout the academic year.

Ongoing throughout the year
