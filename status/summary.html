About Us

Empowering schools in Tanzania with sustainable computer lab solutions.

Last updated: 25/11/2025 - 13:00 EAT

Executive Summary

Reneal, a United States NGO, has been partnering since 2013 with Arusha District to install computer labs in government secondary schools. In 2020, Reneal’s work extended to Longido District, Arusha City Council and expanded further to other districts.

Since 2021 the expansion continued to different regions including Tanga, Dodoma, Pwani, Mara, Mbeya, Lindi and Mtwara. Reneal US relies heavily on Reneal Team Tanzania to execute its programs, a key reason for success to date.

User Features

  • ✔ Applications: Office suite, photo/video editing, webpage design, desktop publishing, CAD, programming, etc.
  • ✔ Educational Programs: Math, science, English, geography tools.
  • ✔ Content Library: Books, movies, offline Wikipedia, past National Exam papers, Tanzania curriculum notes, science simulations stored on server.
  • ✔ Classroom Management: Epoptes allows monitoring, screen sharing, and video streaming.
  • ✔ File Storage: TeachersShared and AllUsersShared areas for permission-controlled content sharing.

System Design

  • ● Linux & Open Source Software architecture.
  • ● Client-Server: All programs run on the server; clients connect via network switch.
  • ● Scalable from 17 seats up to ~40 seats per lab.
  • ● Internet ready (plugs into server) with custom filtering and blocking software.
  • ● Easy Replication: Entire system clonable via USB hard drive.
  • ● Security Levels: System Admin, Teacher, and Student accounts.
